Who am I?
I am a UX Designer who loves to build! Apps, Websites, and even furniture and models! As you might guess, I specialize in product design, wireframing, and prototyping. My goal is to create products that are enjoyable to use and bring peak utility to my users.

My Skills

Creativity

Brainstorming, especially over coffee, is a treat for me. I try to approach problems knowing that more than one solutution can exist. Often nature and craftsmanship from the past are sources of inspiration to me.

Wire-framing & prototyping

I approach wire-framing and prototyping from the perspective that 'less is more'. Sites and apps should be easy for the user to navigate and intuitive to understand. My favorite tools are Figma, Miro, and InVision.

Project Management

I am a planner and logically-minded by nature, so Project management comes easily to me. In my latest project, I facilitated daily stand-ups and assigned task according to team member effieciencies.

My Work

Houndoogle

Houndoogle us an original mobile app deisgned by dog owners for dog owners. It's goal is to make finding vets, parks shops, and other dog-friendly places easier for dog owners.

Department of the Interior Site Redesign

The Dept. of the Interior website embodies the term "goverment issued." I redesigned the site to be more enjoyable to navigate and the capture the spirit of those the DOI serves

S.O.S for Humanitarian Actions Site Redesign

S.O.S for Humanitarian Actions is an Atlanta-based non-profit who's website was sending out an "SOS". I redesigned the site to be more trustworthy and transparent in order to encourage more donations.
